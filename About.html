About Us

Message from Chairman

Experts' Zone is a renowned Science and Commerce Institute of Aga-Khan Board (AKU-EB) that amalgamates some of the brightest students and very passionate teachers, along with the most innovative tools to address basic academic problems and to eliminate the stumbling blocks in the success of our students.


At Experts' Zone , We place great care and effort into offering the very best in instruction by providing students with opportunities in which they can discover untapped strengths and nurture new concepts all under the supervision of a well trained staff.


Our mission and aim is to provide the quality education to the youth of Pakistan, so they can serve our country in future for better tomorrow.

C.E.O - Experts' Zone
Sir Naushad Mehdi
